This Is Who We Are

CodeStallion is a web and mobile development company that seeks to provide scalable solutions to its clients.

On each project we are involved in, we do not work exclusively on our own. We are firm believers in skills transfer and as a result, we have an intake of unemployed computer science graduates from the city our clients are based in to develop and hone their skills.

We employ solutions that evolve with market requirements. We are aware of the changing nature of the IT environement and as a result, we are constantly testing current technologies to tailor our products to the business case in question.

Why We Do What We Do

Starting a company(2018 January) was a result of our obsession with extracting value from the latest technologies in our personal capacities, we seek to bring the same value to end users who are unable to source the same value with existing technologies.

This has resulted in the misuse of each piece of technology by students and learners alike, which consequently costs the sector exorbitant amounts of money for maintanance on an annual basis.
